One Pot Vegetable and Rice


Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, beans, corn, etc.)
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Oil or butter
  • Spices: cumin, turmeric, pepper/chili flakes
  • Salt
  • Water or vegetable broth


Instructions

  1. Heat oil, sauté onions and garlic until soft, then add vegetables and cook briefly until slightly tender and aromatic.
  2. Add rice, spices, and liquid, mix well, then cover and cook on low until the rice is fluffy and fully cooked, letting everything absorb the flavors together.


Tips

  • Use broth instead of water for extra flavor.
  • Don’t stir too much while cooking—keeps rice fluffy.



Veggie Loaded Shakshuka


Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Tomatoes (fresh or canned)
  • Bell peppers, onions, spinach
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Spices: paprika, cumin, chili flakes
  • Salt
  • Fresh herbs (parsley/coriander)

Instructions

  1. Sauté onions, garlic, and vegetables in olive oil until softened, then add tomatoes and spices and cook until the sauce thickens and becomes rich.
  2. Make small wells and crack eggs into the sauce, cover, and cook until the eggs are just set to your liking, then garnish and serve hot.

Tips

  • Keep yolks slightly runny for best texture.
  • Add feta for extra richness.

Cheese and Spinach Quesadillas


Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Paneer (crumbled or cubed)
  • Spinach
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Cheese
  • Oil or butter
  • Spices: chili flakes, cumin, salt

Instructions

  1. Cook onions, garlic, and spinach until wilted, then add paneer and spices, mixing until everything is well combined and flavorful.
  2. Spread the filling (and cheese if using) onto tortillas, fold, and cook on a pan until golden and crisp on both sides.

Tips

  • Cook on medium heat so tortillas crisp without burning.
  • Add extra cheese for better binding.
