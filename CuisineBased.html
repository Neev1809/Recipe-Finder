Mushroom Risotto


Ingredients

  • Arborio Rice
  • Mushrooms
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Butter or Olive Oil
  • Vegetable Broth
  • Parmesan
  • Salt and Pepper


Instructions

  1. Sauté onions, garlic, and mushrooms in butter until soft and fragrant, then add rice and toast lightly to enhance its flavor.
  2. Gradually add warm broth, stirring continuously and allowing it to absorb before adding more, until the rice becomes creamy and cooked through, then finish with butter or cheese.


Tips

  • Stir often for that creamy texture.
  • Always use warm broth—cold slows cooking.

Spanakopita


Ingredients

  • Spinach
  • Feta Cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Phyllo pastry
  • Olive oil or butter
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook spinach with onion and garlic until wilted and dry, then mix with crumbled feta and seasoning to create the filling.
  2. Layer phyllo sheets with oil/butter, spread the filling, fold or layer, and bake until golden, crisp, and flaky.

Tips

  • Keep phyllo covered to prevent drying out.
  • Don’t leave moisture in spinach—makes it soggy.

Ratatouille


Ingredients

  • Eggplant
  • Zucchini
  • Bell Peppers
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Herbs (thyme/basil)
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Sauté onions and garlic, then add tomatoes and herbs to create a flavorful base, cooking until slightly thickened.
  2. Add chopped vegetables and cook slowly on low heat until tender and well combined, allowing the flavors to meld beautifully.

Tips

  • Cook slowly—rushing ruins the flavor.
  • Cut veggies evenly for consistent texture.
