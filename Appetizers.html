Bruschetta


Description

A classic Italian appetizer made with crispy toasted bread topped with a fresh mixture of juicy tomatoes, basil, and garlic. Light, flavorful, and perfect to start any meal.

Ingredients

<

  • 1 baguette or bread loaf (sliced)
  • 3–4 ripe tomatoes (finely chopped)
  • 2–3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh basil leaves (chopped)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black pepper (to taste)


Instructions

  1. Prepare the topping In a bowl, mix chopped tomatoes, garlic, basil, salt, pepper, and 1 tbsp olive oil. Let it sit for 10–15 minutes to enhance the flavor.
  2. Toast the bread Slice the baguette and toast the pieces in a pan or oven until they turn golden and crispy.
  3. Add flavor to bread Lightly rub a garlic clove on the warm toasted bread for extra taste (optional but recommended).
  4. Add flavor to bread Lightly rub a garlic clove on the warm toasted bread for extra taste (optional but recommended).
  5. Final touch Drizzle a little olive oil on top and serve immediately.

  • Use fresh and ripe tomatoes for best taste
  • Serve immediately to keep bread crispy
  • You can add mozzarella or cheese for a richer version

French Fries


Description

Crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, French fries are a classic snack loved by everyone. Perfectly golden and lightly seasoned, they make for the ultimate comfort food and pair well with a variety of dips.

Ingredients

  • 3–4 large potatoes
  • Oil (for frying)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp paprika or chili powder (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the potatoes Wash, peel (optional), and cut the potatoes into thin, even strips.
  2. Soak in water Soak the cut potatoes in cold water for 20–30 minutes to remove excess starch. This helps make them crispier.
  3. Dry completely Drain and pat dry using a clean cloth or paper towel.
  4. First fry (important step) Heat oil on medium heat and fry the potatoes for 4–5 minutes until soft but not brown. Remove and let them rest.
  5. Second fry (for crispiness) Increase heat and fry again until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Season and serve Remove excess oil, sprinkle salt and spices, and serve hot.

Tips

  • Double frying gives perfect crispiness
  • Use starchy potatoes for best results
  • Don’t overcrowd the pan while frying
  • Serve immediately for best taste

Veg Cutlet

Description

A creamy and nutritious Middle Eastern dip made from blended chickpeas, served with soft and warm pita bread. Light, flavorful, and perfect as a healthy appetizer.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup boiled chickpeas
  • 2 tbsp tahini (optional)
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 2–3 tbsp water
  • Pita bread (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Blend the hummus In a mixer, add chickpeas, garlic, lemon juice, tahini, olive oil, salt, and water.
  2. Make it smooth Blend until creamy and smooth. Adjust consistency with water.
  3. Serve Transfer to a bowl, drizzle olive oil on top, and serve with warm pita bread.

Tips

  • Peel chickpeas for extra smooth texture
  • Add paprika or chili oil for flavor
  • Serve slightly chilled

Mini-Sanwiches

Description

Bite-sized sandwiches packed with fresh vegetables and delicious spreads. Perfect for quick snacks, parties, or light meals.

Ingredients

  • Bread slices
  • Butter or mayonnaise
  • 1 cucumber (sliced)
  • 1 tomato (sliced)
  • 1 boiled potato (sliced)
  • Salt & pepper
  • Green chutney (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the bread Apply butter or mayo on bread slices.
  2. Add fillings Layer cucumber, tomato, and potato slices. Sprinkle salt and pepper.
  3. Assemble Cover with another bread slice and press gently.
  4. Cut & serve Cut into small squares or triangles.

Tips

  • Remove edges for a cleaner look
  • Add cheese or sauces for more flavor
  • Keep refrigerated before serving for better texture
