Cake

Description

A soft and fluffy baked dessert loved worldwide. Perfect for celebrations or as a sweet treat.

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C
  2. Mix butter and sugar until creamy
  3. Add eggs and vanilla
  4. Mix dry ingredients and combine with milk
  5. Pour into a baking pan
  6. Bake for 30–35 minutes

Tips

  • Do not overmix batter
  • Check with toothpick before removing
  • Let cake cool before cutting

Brownie

Description

A rich and fudgy chocolate dessert with a soft center and slightly crispy top.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chocolate
  • ½ cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup flour

Instructions

  1. Melt chocolate and butter
  2. Add sugar and eggs
  3. Mix flour
  4. Bake at 180°C for 20–25 mins

Tips

  • Do not overbake
  • Use dark chocolate for better taste
  • Let it cool before slicing

Ice Cream

Description

A creamy frozen dessert that is refreshing and loved by all age groups.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup cream
  • ½ cup sugar
  • Flavor (vanilla/chocolate)

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients
  2. Chill mixture
  3. Freeze and churn every 2 hours

Tips

  • Use full-fat milk for creaminess
  • Churn to avoid ice crystals
  • Add fruits or chocolate chips

Gulab Jamun

Description

A classic Indian dessert made of soft fried balls soaked in sweet sugar syrup.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • Milk (for dough)
  • Sugar syrup
  • Cardamom

Instructions

  1. Prepare soft dough
  2. Make small balls
  3. Fry until golden brown
  4. Soak in warm sugar syrup

Tips

  • Do not over-fry
  • Keep dough soft
  • Soak while warm for best results
