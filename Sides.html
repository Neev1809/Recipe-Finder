Side Dishes

Perfect companions to your main course, adding flavor and variety to your meals.

Salad

Description

A fresh and healthy mix of vegetables that is light, nutritious, and refreshing.

Ingredients

  • Cucumber (chopped)
  • Tomato (chopped)
  • Onion (optional)
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. Chop all vegetables
  2. Add salt, pepper, and lemon juice
  3. Mix well
  4. Serve fresh

Tips

  • Use fresh vegetables
  • Add olive oil for taste
  • Serve immediately

Garlic Bread

Description

Crispy bread topped with flavorful garlic butter, perfect as a side.

Ingredients

  • Bread loaf
  • Butter
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Parsley

Instructions

  1. Mix butter and garlic
  2. Spread on bread
  3. Bake at 180°C for 10 mins

Tips

  • Use fresh garlic
  • Add cheese for variation
  • Serve hot

Mashed Potato

Description

Smooth, creamy, and comforting mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Boil potatoes
  2. Mash them
  3. Add butter and milk
  4. Mix until smooth

Tips

  • Do not overmix
  • Use warm milk
  • Add pepper for flavor

Coleslaw

Description

A crunchy cabbage salad mixed with creamy dressing.

Ingredients

  • Cabbage (shredded)
  • Carrot (grated)
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. Mix cabbage and carrot
  2. Add mayonnaise and seasoning
  3. Toss well
  4. Chill before serving

Tips

  • Refrigerate before serving
  • Do not add too much mayo
  • Add vinegar for tangy taste
